Ingredients

  • 1 Box Barilla® Oven-Ready Lasagne
  • 2 Jars Barilla® Traditional Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 Pound ground beef
  • 1 16-ounce bag shredded mozzarella, divided
  • 8 Fresh basil leaves, cut julienne
  • Discretionary Salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400° F
  2. Sauté ground beef in olive oil until browned and crumbled. Add sauce and 2 cups of water, season with salt and pepper and simmer for 5 minutes
  3. Pour in 1 ½ cups of the meat sauce, top with 3 sheets of lasagne
  4. Top the next layer with 1 ½ cups of meat sauce, 4-ounces of cheese and some basil
  5. Repeat step 4 for each of the three layers
  6. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in oven for 25-30 minutes, remove the foil and top with the remaining cheese
  7. Place back into the oven and let cheese melt. Remove lasagne, then top with basil then let it rest for 10 minutes before serving
